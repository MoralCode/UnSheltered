About Us


This website (maybe app later) was started at NWAPW


The homeless problem in Portland is horribly sad and


is a very grim situation for youth especially. The


goal of this project is to provide a one stop shop for


homeless youth. Information is provided on local shelters


(including current capacities), rights of homeless youth,


and volunteer opportunities to help the homeless.
