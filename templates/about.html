About Us

This website (possibly an app) was started at NWAPW due to the severe homeless problem in Portland. Many homeless youth travel long miles to homeless shelters, just to find out that there are no spots availabe. Therefore, the goal of this project is to provide a one-stop shop for homeless youth. On this website, you can find information about local shelters, the rights of homeless youth, and even volunteer opportunities to help the homeless.

Important:
Many of these shelters now have waiting lists, some of which may be very long. Shelters and services also go out of business. Always call ahead to confirm that a location is still active and accepting.
