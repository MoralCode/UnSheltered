The Rights of Homeless Youth

The McKinney-Vento Act


To benefit from protections of this act you must lack a regular or fixed nightime residence. It also applies to those sharing housing due to a hardship (economic or otherwise) or living in a hotel, car, public space, etc. The rights provided through this act include staying at schoool of origin, going to school without your records, transportion to and from school, and access to the school's programs.
